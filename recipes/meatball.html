Meatball Recipe

Description

Meatballs are tender, round balls made from ground meat and often mixed with seasonings such as salt, pepper, garlic, and herbs. They can be baked, fried, grilled, or simmered in sauces to create delicious and hearty meals. Common types of meat used include beef, pork, and chicken. Meatballs offer a nutritious option due to their protein content, and they are versatile enough to pair with a variety of sides and dishes.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (90% lean)
  • Eggs
  • Onion, finely grated
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Dry herbs (optional, such as parsley or thyme)

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, eggs, grated onion, breadcrumbs, salt, pepper, and dried herbs. Mix until well combined.
  2. Using your hands, form the mixture into small meatballs, about 1/2 inch in diameter.
  3. Arrange the meatballs on a baking sheet or plate.
  4. Bake in an oven preheated to 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes, until they are firm and cooked through.
